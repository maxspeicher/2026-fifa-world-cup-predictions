How expected points work: Each team's xG per match is fed into independent Poisson distributions to produce win/draw/loss probabilities. Expected points = 3×P(win) + 1×P(draw). The top 2 in each group advance automatically; 8 of 12 third-placed teams also qualify.

xG data from the companion Predicted xGoals tracker, sourced from qualifying xG (xGscore.io), editorial predictions (ESPN, Big D Soccer, Bleacher Report), market odds (FanDuel, Polymarket), historical tournament adjustments, and opponent-adjusted defensive calibration.
